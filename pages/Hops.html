Hops We Are Currently Growing

Here at FTB we grow many varieties of hops. We are unique in that we were able to obtain hop varieties that are not able to be grown anywhere but the farm that created them. How are we able to do this you ask? We have devolped a relationship partnership with other hop farms that hold the rights to certain varieties of hops. This includes but not limited to Mosaic, Simcoe, Citra,Galaxy just to name a few. We also are working to develop our own varieties that can be found exclusivly at our farm and our partners farms.
